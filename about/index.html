Our Story

Started With a Ute and a Vision

SecureWorks Group started in 2022 when Marnin Stobbe saw a gap in Perth's outdoor living market. Homeowners wanted quality work, honest advice, and someone who actually showed up when they said they would. Most builders weren't delivering on all three.

What began with patios quickly grew into fencing, decking, and complete backyard transformations. Today we're a multi-division company with a dedicated operations team and experienced installers — but the approach hasn't changed. We listen to what you actually want, design something that works for your space, and build it properly.

We partner with you on function, style, and design that works for how you actually live. The right build turns underused backyard into the room your family lives in most.
