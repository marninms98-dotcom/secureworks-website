Why It Matters

More Than Just a Boundary Line

A good fence does more than mark where your property ends. It defines your outdoor space, provides security for your family and pets, blocks out street noise and wind, and adds genuine value to your home. In Perth's northern suburbs — where blocks are getting smaller and neighbours are getting closer — the right fencing solution is essential to comfortable outdoor living.

Whether you need a full boundary replacement, a pool-compliant barrier that meets WA building regulations, or a statement front fence with an automated gate, we approach every fencing project the same way: assess your site, understand what you need, and recommend the best solution for your block and budget.

Our Fencing Services

Fencing Options for Every Perth Property

We specialise in steel and composite fencing systems that are purpose-built for WA conditions. No timber that warps, splits, or gets eaten by termites. Every option below is low-maintenance and rated for Perth's wind regions.

Colorbond Fencing

Australia's most popular fencing material for good reason. Colorbond steel is termite-proof, fire-resistant, and rated for N2/N3 wind regions. Available in nine colours to complement your home. We install boundary fences, side returns, and front feature panels.

Pool Fencing

WA pool barrier regulations are strict — and they should be. We install compliant pool fencing in glass, aluminium, and tubular steel. Every installation meets AS1926 standards and we coordinate with your council inspection to make sure it passes first time.

Gates & Automation

Sliding gates, swing gates, pedestrian gates — manual or automated. We supply and install gate systems integrated with your fencing, including motors, intercoms, and keypad access. Popular in Hillarys, Mindarie, and Joondalup for added street presence and security.

Privacy Screening

Aluminium slat screening adds a modern look while blocking sightlines from neighbours or the street. Available in a range of Colorbond colours and custom spacings. Often paired with our patio builds for a fully enclosed outdoor living area.

Built for WA

Why Perth Fencing Needs to Be Different

Perth is one of the windiest capital cities in Australia. Standard fencing designs from eastern states suppliers often do not account for the sustained afternoon sea breezes and winter storm gusts that hit properties across the northern corridor from Scarborough to Two Rocks. Every fence we install is rated to the correct wind region for your specific location — N2 for standard suburban, N3 for coastal and exposed sites.

Then there is the climate. Perth's hot, dry summers and sandy soils demand materials that will not rust, warp, or degrade under UV. Colorbond steel with its Activate technology and baked-on paint finish is engineered for exactly these conditions. Combined with galvanised steel posts set in concrete footings designed for WA sand, our fences are built for the long haul — not just the first few years.

We also understand local council requirements across Joondalup, Wanneroo, and Stirling. Front fence height limits, pool barrier compliance, and retaining wall setback rules vary between councils, and we handle these details during the quoting stage so there are no surprises once construction starts.
